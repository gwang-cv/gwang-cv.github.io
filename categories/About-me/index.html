Biography

Ph.D. in computer science, Tongji University, expected July 2016

M.Sc. in computer science, Shandong Normal University, June 2013

B.Sc. in computer science, Shandong Normal University, June 2009

My research interests mainly consist of computer vision and pattern recognition: Feature Extraction & Matching, Point Set Registration, Image Stitching, Biomedical Image Analysis, Kernel Method.
