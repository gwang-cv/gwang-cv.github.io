Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student in CAD Research Center, School of Electronics and Information Engineering of Tongji University (TJU), Shanghai, 201804, P.R. China. I have received my BSc degree and the MSc degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Shandong Normal University (SDNU), Ji’nan, 250000, P.R. China, in 2009 and 2013, respectively. My research interests mainly consist of computer vision and pattern recognition: Feature Extraction & Matching, Point Set Registration, Image Stitching, Biomedical Image Analysis, Kernel Method.
